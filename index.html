Privacy Policy — Sky Jumper

Package: club.adstream.skyjumper
Developer contact: support@example.com

This policy explains what data the Sky Jumper app processes and how it is used.

1. Data we process

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) — used by Google AdMob to display and measure ads.
  • Diagnostics (crash/performance logs) — may be processed by Google Play services.

We do not directly collect personal user data (name, email, etc.).

2. Advertising

The app shows ads via Google AdMob. Ads are initialized only after user consent (GDPR/UMP). You can change your choice anytime via system ad settings and/or the in-app consent prompt.

3. Sharing with third parties

Identifiers and diagnostics may be shared with Google solely to serve, measure, and limit ad frequency.

4. Storage and security

Data is processed by service providers (Google) according to their policies and retention periods.

5. Your choices

You can limit ad personalization in device settings and/or via the in-app consent UI.

6. Contact

Contact us at support@example.com.
